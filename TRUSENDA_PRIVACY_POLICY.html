Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

Trusenda CRM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name: To personalize your experience
  • Email address: For account login and communication
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely

Lead Data You Enter

  • Contact information: Names, emails, phone numbers of your leads
  • Business information: Company names, property requirements, budgets
  • Notes and tasks: Your follow-up notes and scheduled tasks
  • Lead status: Cold, Warm, Hot, Closed status indicators

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information: Device type, iOS version, app version
  • Usage data: Features used, app performance
  • Log data: Error logs for debugging and support

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Trusenda CRM service
  • Sync your data across devices (iOS app and web platform)
  • Send you follow-up reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our app and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and protected:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Database: Stored in secure cloud infrastructure (Neon PostgreSQL)
  • Authentication: Secure login via Netlify Identity
  • Payment data: Processed by Stripe (PCI-compliant), we never store credit cards
  • Access control: Only you can access your leads and data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

We only share your information with:

  • Service providers: Netlify (hosting), Neon (database), Stripe (payments), Resend (emails)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your lead data is private and belongs to you. We use it solely to provide the CRM service.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data anytime in the app
  • Export: Download your data as CSV
  • Delete: Remove individual leads or your entire account
  • Modify: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-out: Control notification preferences in Settings

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Deleted leads: Removed immediately from your account
  • Backups: Backup systems purged within 30 days of deletion

7. Children's Privacy

Trusenda CRM is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability (export your data)
  • Right to object to processing

11. Third-Party Services

Our app uses these third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Netlify: Hosting and authentication - Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing - Privacy Policy
  • Neon: Database hosting - Privacy Policy
  • Resend: Email delivery - Privacy Policy
